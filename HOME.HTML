Common Signs & Symptoms

01

Emotional Symptoms

  • Constant worry
  • Feeling nervous or on edge
  • Fear that’s hard to control
  • Irritability
02

Behavioral Symptoms

  • Avoiding situations
  • Overthinking
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Irritability
03

Physical Symptoms

  • Fast heartbeat
  • Shortness of breath
  • Sweating or shaking
  • Stomach discomfort

Anxiety can affect how you feel, think, and act. These signs and symptoms are common, and experiencing them does not mean something is wrong with you.
